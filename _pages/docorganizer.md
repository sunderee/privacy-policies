## DOCOrganizer Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how DOCOrganizer (the "App") collects, uses, and shares information about you and the steps we take to protect your privacy. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

**1. App Description**

DOCOrganizer is an intuitive document scanning app that enables users to easily scan, store, and manage documents directly from their mobile devices. Designed to work seamlessly across Android and iOS platforms, the App provides a user-friendly interface to create high-quality PDF scans without the need for an internet connection or external services. With DOCOrganizer, your documents remain securely stored on your device, ensuring your information stays private and accessible only to you.

**2. Information Collection and Use**

DOCOrganizer does not require users to create an account, nor does it collect personal information from its users. All data and scanned documents are stored locally on the user's device. The App does not use any external analytics services, ensuring that your data remains private and under your control.

**3. Data Usage**

The App does not collect or use data for any purpose, as all information and documents remain on the device. Users may opt to share PDF scans created by the App, but this action is outside the control and responsibility of DOCOrganizer.

**4. Data Sharing**

DOCOrganizer does not share any data with third parties, as it does not collect user data. Any sharing of documents by the user is done at their discretion and is not facilitated by the App.

**5. User Rights**

Users retain full control over their documents and data within the App. The App does not have the capability to access or manage user data remotely.

**6. Data Storage and Security**

All user data, including scanned documents, are stored locally on the user's device. The App is designed to ensure that users' privacy is maintained, without the need for external data storage or transmission.

**7. Children's Privacy**

The App is intended for use by individuals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 years of age.

**8. International Data Transfer**

There is no international transfer of data, as the App does not collect or store data on remote servers.

**9. Privacy Policy Updates**

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Users are encouraged to review the policy periodically for any changes. Updates will be posted on our website at [https://sunderee.github.io/privacy-policies](https://sunderee.github.io/privacy-policies).
