# NewsY Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how NewsY (the "App") collects, uses, and shares information about you and the steps we take to protect your privacy. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

**1. App Description**

NewsY is a simple, lightweight RSS feed aggregator designed to provide users with a hassle-free way to access and read content from various RSS feeds. The App allows users to add RSS feed URLs to their reading list, offers offline access by caching content, enables users to archive content for later reading, and provides suggested feeds through Firebase Remote Config. The App is developed using Flutter, ensuring a cross-platform experience for both Android and iOS devices.

**2. Information Collection and Use**

NewsY does not require users to create an account, and we do not collect personal information directly from users. However, the App uses Firebase Analytics for analytics purposes, which may collect data as permitted by Google's policies. Additionally, Firebase Remote Config is used to manage suggested feeds.

**3. Data Usage**

The collected data is used solely for analytics purposes to improve the App's functionality and user experience. Since the App does not collect personal information, user activities cannot be personally identified.

**4. Data Sharing**

The only third-party service the App shares data with is Google, through the use of Firebase Analytics. No personal data is shared since the App does not collect such information from its users.

**5. User Rights**

Users have full control over their data within the App, as all activities and data remain on the device. There is no mechanism for the App or its developers to access or manage user data remotely.

**6. Data Storage and Security**

All user data and activities are stored locally on the user's device. The App does not transmit or store data on remote servers, ensuring that users' privacy is maintained.

**7. Children's Privacy**

The App is intended for use by individuals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 years of age.

**8. International Data Transfer**

There is no international transfer of data, as the backend infrastructure is hosted within the EU, and Firebase is configured to store analytics data in the EU as well.

**9. Privacy Policy Updates**

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Users are encouraged to review the policy periodically for any changes. Updates will be posted on our website at [https://sunderee.github.io/privacy-policies](https://sunderee.github.io/privacy-policies).
